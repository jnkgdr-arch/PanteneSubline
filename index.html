Executive Summary

Growth opportunity

Scalp Vitality positions Pantene in the expanding scalp health and technology-driven haircare market by combining tiered light therapy devices, Magnetized Saline Water Ointment, mass-market shampoo, conditioner, and leave-in treatments, digital tracking, and dermatologist-backed service support.

The strategy targets two needs at once: Gen Z consumers seeking prevention, balance, and viral innovation, and 45+ consumers seeking restorative treatment for thinning, density, and age-related scalp decline.

Consultations also create a feedback loop for product effectiveness, helping P&G identify changes needed for devices, ointment pairings, and the broader haircare line after launch.
